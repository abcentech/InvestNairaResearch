FAQ

Questions, answered

What is TREASURE?

TREASURE is a professional market intelligence terminal for Nigerian markets: live NGX equities, FGN bonds and T-bills, FX, macro data, scored news, a factor screener and AI decision support — built entirely on official and exchange sources.

Is TREASURE a Bloomberg alternative for Nigeria?

For Nigerian coverage, yes — it is built to answer the same questions a Bloomberg or Refinitiv terminal answers (valuation vs peers, real yields, breaking market news, audited fundamentals) at a fraction of the cost, with deeper Nigeria-specific data such as parallel-market FX and NGX filings.

Where does the data come from?

Official sources only: the Nigerian Exchange (NGX) daily official lists and filings, the Central Bank of Nigeria, the Debt Management Office, the National Bureau of Statistics, and audited company financial statements. A number that cannot be sourced is shown blank — never estimated.

What are Decision Dossiers?

One auditable answer per security or asset class: six pillars — Valuation, Quality, Momentum, Income, News and vs-Risk-Free — each scored only from real sourced numbers with the evidence printed under every score, plus what the riskless alternative currently pays.

Can I get alerts on Telegram?

Yes. Breaking market-moving news with direction tags, a morning brief, midday and close market pulses, on-demand decision dossiers, plain-English questions to the AI analyst, and a personal radar that watches the names you actually hold.

Who is TREASURE for?

Global investors and funds allocating to Nigeria and frontier Africa, local asset managers and analysts, the Nigerian diaspora managing money home, and serious retail investors who want institutional-grade data instead of rumours.
